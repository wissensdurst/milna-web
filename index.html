We make people genuinely happy

Thx for the fantastic 2 weeks

We have been in Tomislavs place 2 weeks this summer. We are a family with 2 kids age 14 and 16. The location is a 4 minutes drive from Milna in little hidden bay. The bay has a little platform to get easily in the water. This bay is really secluded and a fantastic place to spend an afternoon close to the water without having crowds of people around you. The house or in this case 3 houses - one with kitchen and dining, one with 2 bedrooms and a bathroom and one just a bathroom - are really well equipped with newest appliances, flatscreen and air conditioning, refrigerator with crushed and cubes ice. There is an outside patio with a sun screen to sit and have lunch or dinner, and a separate barbecue site to grill your food. The main house also facilitates a fireplace to grill inside if necessary. Around the pool you will find several sunbeds, lounge chairs and sun screens to relax during the day. The pool is sized large enough to have 4 people inside swimming around without any problems. Behind the house is a little playground for younger kids to have some fun. The estate is covered with olive trees all around and you even have lemon tree spending shadow during the day near the pool. Really remarkable is Tomislavs attention to detail for the little things like light switches where they have to be, little candle lights outside, RGB lighting for the pool and many more. Overall with had fantastic stay there, secluded house, private bay and if you are looking for some entertainment you go to Milna. You find a parking place immediately on the right side near the Marina and all you need to do is walk two minutes and you are among all those great restaurants that Milna offers. Thx for the fantastic 2 weeks!!
